Apply for a grant to build affordable homes

Use this service to apply for grants to build affordable homes through continuous market engagement.

If you’re a consortium lead, you can complete this application for another organisation who is a member of your consortium.

Before you start

You can read guidance on what you’ll need to complete your application.

You'll need to add your sites, then add the schemes within this site to apply for grants for each type of affordable housing.

You can save your application as a draft and complete it in your own time.
Start now

If you need help using this service

Contact your point of contact at Homes England, such as your provider manager or growth manager, if you need help completing your application.

Email Homes England at ahp2126@homesengland.gov.uk if you’re unsure who to contact.
